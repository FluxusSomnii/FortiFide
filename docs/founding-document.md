# Forti Fide — Founding Document

*With strong trust.*

## What it is

Forti Fide is the external instrument of the Fluxus Somnii ecosystem. It makes visible what the information environment does to language in your presence — the rhetorical structure of what arrives at you through audio, annotated in real time, returned to you as self-knowledge.

It captures. It annotates. It stores locally. It reflects patterns over time. It does nothing else.

## The problem it addresses

The systems that deliver your information have a detailed model of your fears, your identity, your attention. They use it continuously. You, by default, have no comparable model of what they are doing to you. Forti Fide closes that gap — not by fighting the systems, not by filtering what arrives, but by returning the structure to you so you can see it.

## The deflattening principle

Five dimensions that the information environment removes from perception:

1. **Register** — the emotional channel being used (Fear, Identity, Authority, Intimacy, Rational)
2. **Pattern** — the specific rhetorical technique being deployed
3. **Confidence** — how certain the detection is
4. **Source** — who is speaking and in what relationship to you
5. **Trajectory** — how your information environment's rhetorical character has changed over time

Forti Fide returns all five.

## The sovereignty principle

Forti Fide does not decide what you should believe or consume. It does not filter your feed or block content. It does not tell you what conclusions to draw. It annotates what arrived and returns that annotation to you. What you do with what you see is entirely and only yours.

## What it refuses to do

- No blocking or filtering
- No telling you what to conclude
- No cloud processing — Whisper runs on your device
- No account required
- No advertising, no data sales, no telemetry
- No score, no grade, no wellness rating
- No editorial layer deciding what gets flagged

## The glass box

Every pattern Forti Fide can detect is readable inside the app before any session begins. The detection logic is in the repository. The pattern library is open and community-governed. No single entity controls what gets flagged or how. The instrument cannot be tuned to serve undisclosed interests. That constraint is structural, not instructional.
---

*The instrument does not coerce. It returns awareness. What the user does with that awareness is entirely and only theirs.*
