# Forti Fide — Terms of Use

*Effective date: upon first release.*

## What Forti Fide is

Forti Fide is a free, open source desktop application for rhetorical awareness, released under GPL v3.

## The license

GPL v3. You can use, modify, and distribute Forti Fide. Any derivative must also be GPL v3. You cannot distribute Forti Fide under a closed source or proprietary license.

## Ambient recording — your responsibility

Recording laws vary by jurisdiction. In many places, recording a conversation requires the consent of all parties. You are responsible for understanding and complying with the recording laws that apply in your location. Forti Fide displays a clear indicator when recording is active.

## No warranty

Forti Fide is provided as-is. Pattern detection is probabilistic. Confidence scores indicate likelihood, not certainty. A high-confidence annotation is not proof that a specific rhetorical intent existed.

Donations are voluntary and non-refundable. A donation does not create any entitlement to support, features, or service commitments.
